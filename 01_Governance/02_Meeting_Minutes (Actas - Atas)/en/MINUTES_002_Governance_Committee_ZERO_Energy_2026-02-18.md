# MINUTES Nº 002
**ZERO Energy Governance Committee**

* **Date:** February 18, 2026
* **Time:** 09:00 AM
* **Modality:** Virtual meeting

---

### 1. Punctuality and session access rules
The need to guarantee punctuality and respect for the established schedules in internal meetings, training, and project presentations is reinforced.
It is agreed to start all sessions punctually, deny access to those who join more than five minutes late without prior notice, allow access only in case of justified prior notice, and require the organizing team to connect at least fifteen minutes in advance.

### 2. Internal communication and response times
A clear communication framework is established to avoid operational blockages.
The operational team maintains an indicative maximum response time of up to three hours, with the obligation of prior notice in case of temporary unavailability. The Governance Committee and Councilors have a maximum response time of up to twenty-four hours. Repeated failure to respond may be considered a lack of operability.

### 3. Schedule review and session attendance
A progressive decrease in attendance at certain community sessions is noted. It is agreed to review and adjust current schedules, working with morning, afternoon, and evening slots, facilitating participation from different time zones.

### 4. Calls, calendar, and channel usage
The meeting call system is reviewed with the aim of reducing message saturation. Calls must be communicated further in advance, avoiding unnecessary duplications and ordering reminders on the same day of the session.

### 5. General operation of tokens and balance
Operational aspects related to the use of tokens and balance within the platform are clarified, aimed at ensuring the sustainability of the project, facilitating the incorporation of new users, and incentivizing reinvestment and growth. Explanatory documentation will be prepared.

### 6. Minutes, bylaws, and documentary transparency
The project's commitment to transparency is ratified. Official minutes will be progressively published in a public GitHub repository. Bylaws will be made public once formalized and signed, allowing read-only access.

### 7. Approval and adjournment
The agreements gathered in these minutes are approved by consensus of the Governance Committee. The session is adjourned.
