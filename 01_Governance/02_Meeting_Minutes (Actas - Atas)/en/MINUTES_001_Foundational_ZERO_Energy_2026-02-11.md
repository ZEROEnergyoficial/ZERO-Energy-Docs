# MINUTES Nº 001
**ZERO Energy Governance Committee**

* **Date:** February 11, 2026
* **Time:** 09:00 AM
* **Modality:** Virtual meeting

---

### 1. Formal constitution of the Committee
The first formal session of the ZERO Energy Governance Committee is held with the objective of establishing the initial organizational framework of the project in its pre-DAO phase. The Governance Committee Bylaws are read and reviewed, being approved as the foundational document regulating the internal functioning of the body.
Its nature is established as: Internal, Strategic, Collegiate, and Transitional, with planned evolution towards a decentralized governance model (DAO).

### 2. Approved organizational structure
The following organizational structure is ratified:
* **Governance Committee:** Councilors, Delegated Councilor, Secretary, and Treasurers.
* **Operational Team:** Systems Direction, Community and Expansion, Training and Events, Automation and AI, Technological Development, and other roles that may be defined based on the project's needs.

The possibility of temporary accumulation of positions in the initial phase is established.

### 3. General remuneration regime
A compensation model based on tokenized merits is approved for governance positions, a hybrid model (USDC + merits) or USDC remuneration for operational roles depending on the function, non-automatic nor indefinite nature of the remunerations, and subject to future review according to the economic evolution of the project. Specific conditions are developed in internal appendices.

### 4. Recognition of historical merits
Formal recognition of contributions, work, and risks assumed in previous phases of the project is agreed upon. These merits will be documented in a specific appendix and form part of the foundational framework.

### 5. Treasury and multisig system
The implementation of a multi-signature (multisig) system for the management of project funds is approved, ensuring security, traceability, and collegiate control. It is agreed to formally identify the treasurers and establish the corresponding procedures.

### 6. Future transition model to DAO
It is noted that the Governance Committee is transitional and may evolve or integrate into a DAO structure as the project develops. No perpetual automatic rights derived from the current configuration are established.

### 7. Operational organization and work dynamics
It is agreed to structure the community and training activity through a weekly promotion and tracking session, a weekly presentation and practical work session, and additional specific sessions when necessary.

### 8. Approval
The Bylaws and the initial organizational framework of the ZERO Energy Governance Committee are approved by majority. The session is adjourned.
