# MINUTES Nº 005
**ZERO Energy Governance Committee**

* **Date:** March 11, 2026
* **Time:** 09:00 AM

---

### 1. Opening of the session
The Governance Committee session begins with the objective of reviewing the evolution of the ZERO Energy project, analyzing proposals related to community development, and coordinating strategic actions for the next phases of the ecosystem.
During the session, operational aspects of the project, event organization, community evolution, and possible growth strategies were reviewed.

### 2. Organization and participation in industry events
Team participation in an upcoming industry event was reviewed.
* Coordination of team members' attendance.
* Ticket management for participants.
* Exploring the possibility of centralizing ticket purchases to facilitate logistics.
* Search for joint accommodation to optimize group organization.

### 3. Organizational documentation and signing of bylaws
The possibility of taking advantage of the in-person meeting planned at the event to advance the signing of organizational documentation was raised.
It was positively assessed to review and formalize certain organizational documents and digitize them subsequently for archiving.

### 4. Recording and documentation of meetings
It is confirmed that Governance Committee meetings are recorded to facilitate the subsequent drafting of minutes.
The minutes published in the project's repository do not include sensitive information or personal/financial data.

### 5. Community growth strategy
During the session, the recent evolution of the community and the pace of new member incorporation were analyzed.
The importance of reinforcing duplication and training processes to help new participants better understand the tools available was highlighted.

### 6. Development of training materials
The creation of a starter guide for new members was proposed, with the goal of clearly explaining the first steps within the project, the use of available tools, and best practices for community growth.

### 7. Network visibility strategy
Different ways to improve the project's visibility on social media were discussed, encouraging community participation in disseminating informative content and generating activity on personal networks.

### 8. Upcoming strategic project phases
Possible lines of work for the next phases of the project were reviewed, including the progressive development of ecosystem tools, international community expansion, and participation in industry events.

### 9. Recognition of community participation
The possibility of implementing recognition mechanisms for active community members via visual materials and publications that celebrate achievements within the ecosystem was discussed.

### 10. Monitoring community activity
Examples of initiatives driven by community members to support new participants and strengthen collaborative learning within the project were shared.

### 11. Adjournment
The meeting concludes by thanking committee members for their participation and highlighting the importance of continuing to work cooperatively for the project's development.
