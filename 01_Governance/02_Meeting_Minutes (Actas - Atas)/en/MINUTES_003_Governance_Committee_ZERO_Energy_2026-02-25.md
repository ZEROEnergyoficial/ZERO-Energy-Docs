# MINUTES Nº 003
**ZERO Energy Governance Committee**

* **Date:** February 25, 2026
* **Time:** 09:00 AM
* **Duration:** 1h 30 min
* **Modality:** Virtual meeting

---

### 1. Opening of the session
The Governance Committee session begins with the objective of reviewing the evolution of the project and analyzing various proposals related to the development of the community and the organization of ecosystem activities.

### 2. Participation in industry events
The committee analyzes the desirability of participating in events related to the blockchain technology and digital assets sector.
**Agreement:**
It is considered positive for the project to have a presence in this type of gathering for the following reasons:
* Strengthening the project's visibility
* Establishing contacts with industry professionals
* Accessing networking spaces with other technological projects
The committee approves exploring opportunities to participate in relevant industry events.

### 3. Community growth strategy
Various proposals aimed at growing the project ecosystem are reviewed.
**Conclusion:**
The committee reaffirms that the development of the project must focus on:
* Progressive expansion of the community
* Strengthening the network of participants
* Consolidation of the growth model
The importance of maintaining simple and replicable processes that facilitate the participation of new members is emphasized.

### 4. Organization of meetings and presentations
The possibility of organizing in-person meetings to present the project is analyzed.
**Agreement:**
The committee considers it appropriate to begin with small-format events, aimed at presenting the project and facilitating exchange among participants.
This type of gathering will allow:
* Strengthening the community
* Generating spaces for dialogue
* Presenting the project to new interested parties.

### 5. Development of informative content
The importance of having clear and accessible informative materials to facilitate the understanding of the project is discussed.
**Agreement:**
Work will continue on the preparation of presentation materials and informative resources that allow the functioning of the project to be explained simply.

### 6. Monitoring the development of the project
The committee reviews the general evolution of the project and its current state.
It is noted that the project continues to advance in its development and consolidation phase, with the objective of progressively expanding its community and improving its communication tools.

### 7. Adjournment
There being no further business, the Governance Committee session is adjourned.
